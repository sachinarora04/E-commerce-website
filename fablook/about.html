Fablook is an electronic commerce company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka. It was founded in 2007 by Sachin Arora,Shahshank khendelwal,devanshi kackar. The company is registered in Singapore. Fablook has launched its own product range under the name "DigiFlip" with products including tablets, USBs, and laptop bags. According to Morgan Stanley the current market value of Fab look is $5.54 billion as of November 2016.


HISTORY

 

    Fab Look  was founded in 2007 by Sachin arora and shahshank, both alumni
    of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. They worked for Amazon.com,
    and left to create their new company incorporated in October 2007 as Fablook
    Online Services Pvt. Ltd. The first product they sold was the book Leaving
    Microsoft To Change The World to a customer from Hyderabad.Fab look now
    employs more than 33,000 people.After the success of its 2014 Big Billion
    Sale, Fab look carried out a second Big Billion Sale. where it is reported
    that they saw a business turnover of $300 million in gross merchandise volume.



Business Structure

 
    In a report dated 25 November 2014, a leading media outlet reported that
    Fablook were operating through a complex business structure which included
    nine firms, some registered in Singapore and some in India. In 2012 Fablook
    co-founders sold WS Retail to a consortium of investors led 
    by Rajeev Kuchhal.


Acquisitions


2010: WeRead, a social book discovery tool.

2011: Mime360, a digital content platform company.

2011: Chakpak.com, a Bollywood news site that offers updates, news, photos and
      videos. Fab look acquired the rights to Chakpak's digital catalogue which
      includes 40,000 filmographies,  10,000 movies and close to 50,000 ratings.
      Fab look has categorically said that it will not be involved with the	
      original site and will not use the brand name.

2012: Letsbuy.com, an Indian e-retailer in electronics. Fab look has bought the
      company for an estimated US$25 million. Letsbuy.com was closed down and all
      traffic to Letsbuy has been diverted to Fab look.

2014: Acquired Myntra.com in an estimated ?20 billion (US$300 million) deal.

2015: Fab look acquired a mobile marketing start-up Appiterate as to strengthen
      its mobile platform.

2016: Fab look’s Myntra acquires rival fashion shopping site Jabong for $70
      million.

2016: In April, Fab look acquired payment start-up PhonePe.

2017: In January, Fab look funded Parenting Network Tinystep With $2 Million.
